# Release Schedule

Here is the frequency where the team **aim** to cut new releases. As we
strive for quality rather than frequency the date may slip according
to needs.

- Trunk: every 2 weeks
- 1.6: every 4 weeks
- 1.4: every 6 weeks
- 1.2: every 8 weeks
- 1.0: every 10 weeks
